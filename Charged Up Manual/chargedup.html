Game Overview

Each team brings energy to their community by retrieving the game pieces from the substations and scoring it in their grid. Humans will provide the game pieces to the robot from the substations. In the final moments, alliance robots race to dock or engage with the charge station.

  • Total Game Time: 2 minutes 30 seconds
    • Match begins with a 15-second autonomous period and earn points by:
      • Leaving their community,
      • Retrieving and scoring game pieces onto the grid,
      • Docking on or engaging with their charge station.
    • In the final 2 minutes and 15 seconds, drivers take control of robots and score points by:
      • Retrieving and scoring game pieces onto the grid,
      • Docking or engaging charge station
    The alliance with the highest score at the end of the match wins!

    Field Overview
    • Field Size approx.: 26ft. 3.5in (~802cm) by 54ft. 3.25in (~1654cm)

      • 3 red GRIDS and 3 blue GRIDS

      • 1 red Double Substation and 1 blue Double Substation (located)
      • 2 Barriers, 1 separating each community from the opposing alliance’s loading zone
    Gaurd Rails
    • Dimension: 1 ft. 8 in. (~51 cm) tall
    • Made of transparent polycarbonate supported by aluminum extrusion
    • 4 gates in the guardrail that are 3ft. 2 in. (~97 cm) wide
    HDPE Cable Protector
    • Dimension: 5 ft. 6in. (~168 cm) long, 7 in. (~18 cm) wide, ⅞ in. (~22 mm) tall, with ~45° ramps
    • Extends from the guardrail on the scoring table side of the FIELD to the center of each Charge Station
    Area, Zone, Markings
    Barrier
    • Dimensions: 7 ft. 4 in. (~224 cm) long with a base that is 1 ft. 4 in. (~41 cm) wide and ¼ in. (~6 mm) tall
    • Base supports a ½ in. (~13 mm) thick, 1 ft. ¼ in. (~31 cm) tall polycarbonate wall.
    • Separates each COMMUNITY from its adjacent LOADING ZONE.
    Charge Station
    • Dimensions: 8 ft. 1 ¼ in long, 6 ft. 4⅛ in wide, 9⅛ in tall
    • Pivoting Surface of 8ft by 4ft
    • Pivots +/- 15°
    • When Idle, it is at a level position
    • Light turns on when Charge Station is level
